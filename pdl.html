How it works
From road to report
in four steps
01
🎥
Record or upload
Drive normally. Record a 10–30 second clip of any road, or upload from your gallery. GPS coordinates are captured automatically.
02
⚡
AI analyzes frames
YOLOv8 processes every frame, drawing precise bounding boxes around potholes with confidence scores in real time.
03
📍
Pin to the map
Detected potholes are geotagged and added to the live crowdsourced map. Severity is auto-classified: minor, moderate, or severe.
04
🏆
Earn points
Get 10 points per pothole detected. Climb the leaderboard, unlock badges, and build your city's road health score.
